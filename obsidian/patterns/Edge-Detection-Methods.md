# Edge Detection Methods

## 🕵️ **Finding Early Information Sources**

### Platform Hierarchy (Signal Quality):
1. **Discord Alpha Groups** - Highest signal, lowest noise
2. **Small Substack Writers** - <1000 subscribers, high engagement
3. **Academic Twitter** - PhD students, researchers
4. **Niche Reddit** - Specialized communities, sort by new
5. **Conference Speakers** - Before mainstream adoption

### Reverse Engineering Winners:
1. **Find winning trade post-fact**
2. **Track back to earliest mention**
3. **Follow that account religiously**
4. **Study their information sources**

### Capital Flow Tracking:
1. **13F filings** - 45 day delay but shows institutional rotation
2. **ETF fund flows** - Money moving before price
3. **Insider trading filings** - Form 4s, executives buying/selling
4. **Bond spreads** - Credit markets lead equity

## 📊 **Prediction Market Edge**

### Information Asymmetry:
- **Geographic arbitrage** - European/Asian sources ahead of US
- **Industry insiders** - People with domain expertise
- **Academic research** - Studies before mainstream coverage
- **Government connections** - Policy knowledge before announcements

### Whale Behavior Patterns:
- **Position sizing** - How much confidence do they have?
- **Timing patterns** - When do they usually enter/exit?
- **Market selection** - What types of markets do they dominate?
- **Win rates** - Track performance over time

### Platform Inefficiencies:
- **New market premium** - First markets often mispriced
- **Low liquidity** - Small markets easier to move
- **User base bias** - Platform demographics affect pricing
- **Resolution mechanics** - Understanding how markets settle

## 🎯 **Consensus Breaking Points**

### When 10+ Influencers Agree:
- **Trade is crowded** - Edge diminishing
- **Look for contrarian angle** - What are they missing?
- **Time decay** - How long until consensus changes?
- **Next narrative** - What comes after this trade?

### Early Signal Indicators:
- **Sudden follower growth** - Someone called something big
- **Cross-platform confirmation** - Same info on multiple channels
- **Academic paper citations** - Research being weaponized
- **Conference mentions** - Ideas spreading from institutions

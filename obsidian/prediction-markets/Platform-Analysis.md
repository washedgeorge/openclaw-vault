# Prediction Market Platform Analysis

## 🏟️ **Major Platforms**

### Polymarket
- **Focus:** Politics, current events, crypto
- **User Base:** Crypto natives, traders
- **Liquidity:** High on major markets
- **Edge Opportunities:** New markets, low-volume trades
- **Notable Whales:** Track large position holders

### Kalshi
- **Focus:** Regulated, broader topics
- **User Base:** More mainstream, less crypto-focused  
- **Liquidity:** Growing, CFTC regulated
- **Edge Opportunities:** Less sophisticated user base
- **Information Sources:** Traditional media, government data

### Metaculus
- **Focus:** Long-term forecasting, scientific questions
- **User Base:** Researchers, rationalists
- **Scoring:** Track record weighted
- **Edge Opportunities:** Domain expertise in science/tech
- **Top Forecasters:** Identify consistently accurate predictors

## 🐋 **Whale Tracking Strategy**

### Identification Methods:
1. **Large position notifications** - Platform alerts
2. **Unusual market movements** - Price changes without news
3. **Cross-platform activity** - Same wallet/user on multiple platforms
4. **Social media correlation** - Twitter accounts with positions

### Tracking Systems:
- **Wallet addresses** - On-chain analysis for crypto platforms
- **Username correlation** - Same handles across platforms  
- **Timing patterns** - When do they typically trade?
- **Market selection** - What types of questions do they dominate?

### Performance Metrics:
- **Win rate** by market category
- **Average position size** and confidence correlation
- **Hold duration** - Do they trade or hold to resolution?
- **Information sources** - Where do they get edge?

## 🎯 **Edge Opportunities**

### Market Inefficiencies:
1. **New market mispricing** - First 24 hours often wrong
2. **Low liquidity premium** - Small markets easier to predict
3. **Time zone advantages** - Information arbitrage
4. **Domain expertise** - Subject matter knowledge edge

### Contrarian Indicators:
- **Consensus overconfidence** - Markets >90% or <10%
- **Media narrative lag** - Markets slow to adjust to new info
- **Platform user bias** - Demographic skews in pricing

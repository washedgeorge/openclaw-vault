According to all past cycles and records, crops should be larger in
1942, and probably much larger by 1944. If war should last to 1944.
as Government leaders think, and cotton prices remain at high levels.
the cotton production is sure to increase, no matter what the Govern-
ment does to curtail it. If the farmer can sell cotton at 16 to 18 cents.

he will produce enough to force prices down to 8 cents, or probably 5
cents again.

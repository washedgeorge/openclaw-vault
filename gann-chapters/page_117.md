You should also study and watch the comparisons between Wheat

and Corn at extreme high and low levels and note when Corn shows

stronger up trend than Wheat, or stronger down trend. In this way

you will be able to catch some of the important moves and make

larger profits trading in Corn at certain times and in Wheat at others.

Always trade with the main trend.

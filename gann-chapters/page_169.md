1922—-November Low 5534. Making a Double Bottom and a strong
buying level. This was the secondary reaction, making it safer
to buy. From 50 to 70 the half-way point was 60. There was
one Top at 64% and one Top at 651%. Crossing these levels
indicated higher. The advance continued, prices crossed 70, the
old High, and later crossed 7634, the High of March. 1921, indi-
cating higher prices.

1923—June High 8444. This was an Old Top and Bottom level.
Natural resistance point. 75% advance on 50 would give 8714.
Market reaching High in the month of June, the month of the
seasonal change in trend for Tops. You should have sold out
and gone short.

1923—-July Low 54%. The same low as January, 1923. A buying
point because it was down to resistance levels. From 6414 to
8414 would make 741% the half-way point. Crossing this level
indicated stronger position and, when it advanced above it, it
stayed above it. Should have bought more at this level. Later
prices crossed the Tops of 8414, showing strong uptrend.

1924—-June High 9914 was one-half of 199, the high selling price. making
this a resistance level.
July Low 84. Down to OLD TOPS levels and resistance points
and a buying level. The advance was resumed and prices ad-
vanced, crossing $1.00 and later crossing $1.09%, the 50% point
between 1914 and 199.

1924—August High 122. Note 124% was the half-way point between
199 and 50c, making this a selling level.
October Low 104. Holding above $1.00 was a strong indication
for buying. Note that 10574 was three-eighths of 50 to 199,
making this the buying level. Advance was resumed.

1924-December High 133. December was the month for BOTTOMS

due to a very short crop. When prices were high in December.

13254 was two-thirds of 199,
13154 was five-eighths between 199 and 1914,
making this a selling level for reaction.

SWING CHARTS

Swing Charts are very valuable. The best kind to keep for daily
moves is a chart which records all reverse moves or reactions that
run 2 to 3 days; then after there has been a series of advances or up-
swings, the first time prices break below the last SWING BOTTOM
on the 2 or 3-day chart, it is indication the trend is changing.

When a market is declining and has run for a considerable length
of time, making lower tops and lower bottoms on the 2 or 3-day
chart, the first time it crosses the last high on this SWING CHART,
it is an indication that the trend is changing and that a further
rally will take place.

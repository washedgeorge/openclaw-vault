1935—April High 92. Making the third Lower Top. Nearer the time

1935—July Low 5614. Down to an old Bottom and Top Level. Note
that 5814 was the half-way point with a 50% reaction point
between 9314 and 23%, making this a safe, sure buying level.
The market remained in a narrow trading range for several
months, making slightly higher Bottoms and receiving better
support. This was the year of dust storms and a severe drought.
Corn crops were the smallest for many years. A rapid advance
followed. Prices crossed the half-way point between 9314 and
56, indicated higher, and later crossed Tops of 93, advancing
rapidly.

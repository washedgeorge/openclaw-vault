COMMODITIES THAT FAIL TO BREAK OLD BOTTOMS IN A BEAR MARKET.
There are often a few commodities in a Bear market that are strong.
While they may not advance against the trend. however. in time some
of them go exactly opposite the trend. They fail to break old bottoms.
These are not the kinds of commodities to SELL SHORT, because they
are in a stronger position. Always select the commodities to SELL
SHORT that are going with the trend in a Bear market. Our rule
is that you can make the most money trading with the trend. Some-
times the corn crop may be very short and wheat crop very large. Then
corn will have a big advance while wheat declines or holds in a narrow
range.

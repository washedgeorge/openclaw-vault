GAPS AND DAILY LIMITS
At the time of this writing, the LIMIT of fluctuations on Soy Beans
in any one day is 10c per bushel, which means that the price can
advance or decline 10c from the closing price of the previous day.
The LIMIT on Wheat is 8c per bushel each day.
When prices move up the LIMIT in one day, it is the result of in-
creased demand and heavy buying both for long and short accounts.

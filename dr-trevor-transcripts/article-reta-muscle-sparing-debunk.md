# Reta's Big Promise: Lose One-Third of Body Weight — But at What Cost?

*Source: Stay Curious Metabolism (Substack), Feb 2026*
*URL: https://staycuriousmetabolism.substack.com/p/retas-big-promise-lose-one-third*

## Key Claims Debunked

1. **"One-third weight loss in 6 months"** — Overstated. Phase II NEJM data shows -24.2% at 48 weeks. Women subgroup was -28.5%. "One-third" is compound rounding.

2. **"Muscle sparing"** — NOT supported by published data. Lancet Diabetes & Endocrinology sub-study shows:
   - 14 lbs lean mass lost in 32 weeks (8mg and 12mg groups)
   - **37-40% of total weight lost was lean mass** (largely muscle)
   - This is comparable to other GLP-1s (25-39% lean mass loss range)
   - Eli Lilly assisted in writing the manuscript

3. **Huberman called it a "trillion-dollar drug"** for dramatic weight loss + "some degree of muscle sparing." Author couldn't find data to support muscle sparing claim.

## Author's Conclusion
- Reta is NOT muscle sparing vs other GLP-1 RAs based on published data
- Bodybuilders MAY preserve lean mass with resistance training + high protein + anabolics, but that's not the drug doing it
- The drug drives significant weight loss, but ~38% of it is lean mass

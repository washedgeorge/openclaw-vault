## Key Takeaways

### Mars is the trigger planet — confirmed by data
- Mars-Saturn conjunction = **strongest bearish signal** (-6.10% avg 7d, only 30% bullish)
- Mars-Jupiter conjunction = also bearish (-3.46% avg)
- Mars conjunctions with malefics = sell signals
- But Mars sextiles/trines to slow planets = bullish
- This aligns perfectly with Gann/astro tradition: Mars activates, direction depends on aspect type

### Saturn-Pluto = consistently bearish
- Square: -2.64% avg 7d, only **20% bullish** (strongest directional win rate)
- This pair = restriction + destruction. Markets hate it.

### Jupiter-Pluto conjunction = stealth killer
- Only 11 occurrences but **-10.63% avg 14d return** and 27% bullish
- Expansion meets destruction = over-leveraged blowups

### Jupiter-Saturn conjunction = reliably bullish
- +3.12% avg 7d, 65% bullish
- "Great Conjunction" — new economic cycles begin here

### Saturn-Neptune conjunction = neutral (surprising)
- -0.01% avg 7d — basically flat
- BUT: this is because direction varies. The energy is about dissolution/restructuring, not inherently bullish or bearish. The CONTEXT determines direction.

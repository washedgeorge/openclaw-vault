## Gann Key Concepts

### Time Cycles
- **Master Time Factor**: 360 days/degrees (1 year ≈ 1 full zodiacal cycle)
- **Square of 9**: converts price to time using geometric spirals
- **Cardinal dates**: solstices, equinoxes mark major trend changes
- **Planetary pairs**: conjunctions, oppositions, squares of slow planets = major market turns

### Solar Degrees on Bitcoin
- Price converted to degrees on the zodiac wheel
- Aspects between price-degree and planetary positions signal reversals

### 90° Dial
- Compresses 360° into 90° to reveal hidden aspects
- Hard aspects (conjunction, square, opposition) show on same axis
- Used to overlay natal charts and find resonance points

### Lunar Cycles
- New Moon: seed phase, often marks lows or trend starts
- Full Moon: culmination, often marks highs or reversals
- Eclipse: amplified New/Full Moon — effects last weeks
- Monthly lunar cycle overlaid on price for short-term timing

### Planetary Hierarchy (Influence Strength)
1. **Pluto** — generational, complete transformation (245yr cycle)
2. **Neptune** — dissolution, illusion, 165yr cycle
3. **Uranus** — shock, disruption, innovation, 84yr cycle
4. **Saturn** — structure, restriction, reality, 29yr cycle
5. **Jupiter** — expansion, optimism, 12yr cycle
6. **Mars** — energy, aggression, trigger planet (2yr cycle)
7. **Sun/Moon/Eclipses** — timing triggers

### Key Aspects
- **Conjunction (0°)**: fusion of energies, new cycle begins
- **Opposition (180°)**: culmination, tension, reversal
- **Square (90°)**: friction, forced action, volatility
- **Trine (120°)**: flow, support, continuation
- **Sextile (60°)**: opportunity, mild positive

## Trading Framework

### How to Use Astro for Trading
1. **Identify major upcoming aspects** (especially slow planet conjunctions/squares/oppositions)
2. **Check eclipses** — which natal charts do they activate?
3. **Look for convergence** — multiple signals pointing to same date = higher probability
4. **Mars as trigger** — slow planets set the stage, Mars crossing the degree activates the event
5. **Don't trade astro alone** — combine with price action, support/resistance, volume
6. **Energy interpretation** — astro tells you WHEN energy shifts, not always WHICH direction

### Olga Morales / Gann Approach
- Overlay planetary positions on price charts using degree-based conversions
- Use squares and triangles as geometric timing tools
- Cardinal ingresses (0° Aries/Cancer/Libra/Capricorn) are master timing points
- Retrograde periods = review/reversal energy, not always bearish
